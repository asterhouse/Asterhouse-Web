About Us

Imagine a 19 year old Jim Morrison/Robert Plant/Roger Daltrey/Kurt Cobain Writing for a 15 year old Jimmy Page/Pete Townsend/Tom Morello on Guitar, 18 year old John Entwistle/Bill Wyman on Bass and a 19 Year old John Bonham/Kieth Moon/Mike Portnoy on Drums while still pulling off the lead vocals at the same time, much like Mickie Dolenz (the monkeys), Levon Helm (the band), Don Henley (the eagles), Phil Colins (solo/genesis), but sounding closer to a Billy Corgan of Smashing Pumpkins.

John



John Thornburg is a master of vocals and drums and can often be seen hanging out at Chet's By The Bay in Edmonds with his long time friend Chet Stanton.

Russ



Russ Thornburg has been playing guitar since the uterus and loves to play loud and proud. He's recorded a few solo project and recently lost his life-long partner, Holly.

Julio



Julio Posada has his own style of playing bass and loves eating granola bars during shows.
