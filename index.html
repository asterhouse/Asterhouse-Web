About

Asterhouse is a rock band formerly known as JAR. Raised on a healthy diet of The Doors, Smashing Pumpkins, and Incubus, the Thornburg brothers have been making music together since they were young kids. They later met Julio to form JAR and now Asterhouse.

They have been playing loud and energetic from the beginning. However, recently theyâ€™ve been exploring a calmer sound of music more along the lines of Elliott Smith and Wilco. As a result, their songs are made up of a variety of different sounds and moods to fit every occasion.

Russ



Russ Thornburg has been shredding for over a decade and is still under 20. His skills are far beyond his years and with role models like Jimmy Page and Jim Morrison, he has a sound like nothing else, and always turns the amp to 11.

John



John Thornburg has been rocking on the drums from a really young age, growing up with bands like Wilco and The Doors. With musical influences like Billy Corgan and Bob Dylan, he has created a style that can only be described as John Thornburg.

Julio



Julio Posada has a sound of his own from the influences of John Entwistle, Ben Kenny, and Trent Reznor. Growing up with bands like Incubus, Nine Inch Nails and Type O Negative, he has only played for 6 years and already has fluency of bassists many years older.
